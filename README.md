---

## 💡 The Vision

A.R.C.H.I.E. isn't just another dashboard; it’s my attempt at building a **Real-World Jarvis**. I wanted to move away from the "chat-box" era of AI and move into the "Agentic" era. A.R.C.H.I.E. is designed to be a **Visual Command Center**—a high-intelligence partner that doesn't just talk to you, but visualizes the world, monitors global trends, and thinks out loud.

I built this because I believe the tools we use to study and work should feel *alive*.

## 🛠 The "Free-Forever" Tech Stack

I’m a big believer in accessible tech. I wanted to prove you can build a high-end AI assistant for **$0 operation costs**.

- **Frontend:** React / Next.js via Bolt.new (for speed and scalability).
- **Styling:** Tailwind CSS with a custom **Cyber-Glassmorphism** theme.
- **Ears & Voice:** Native **Web Speech API** (Zero-cost, browser-native voice recognition and synthesis).
- **Intelligence:** Puter.js / Gemini API (Free tier logic).
- **Memory:** Supabase (To keep A.R.C.H.I.E. from "forgetting" who you are).

---

## 🛰 The 6 Core Sectors

A.R.C.H.I.E. is divided into specialized modules that act as a "Global HUD":

1. **🌍 World:** Real-time visualization of global events. Ask what’s happening, and Archie maps it out.
2. **📈 Finance:** Market trends, crypto volatility, and economic shifts visualized through dynamic widgets.
3. **🧪 Tech:** The latest in AI, hardware, and software breakthroughs.
4. **🚩 Region:** A localized deep-dive into your specific country or nation of choice.
5. **🖥 Mainframe:** The raw, agentic brain. This is where you give Archie complex tasks to solve.
6. **⚙️ Settings:** Full control over UI, voice speed, and the "Light/Dark" mode toggle.

---

## 🔥 Advanced Agentic Features

I didn't stop at just a dashboard. I’ve integrated some "Tony Stark" level features:

- **Omni-Channel Voice:** You can literally talk to the UI. Archie listens, processes, and speaks back using browser-native synthesis.
- **The Galaxy Map:** A central "Thought Node" visualization. As you speak, Archie maps out your keywords as glowing nodes in a central galaxy.
- **Fast vs. Master Mode:** Switch between "Fast" (for quick facts) and "Master" (for deep, Socratic tutoring and complex reasoning).
- **Internal Thought Terminal:** A real-time log showing Archie’s "Internal Monologue"—watch the AI solve problems before it gives you the answer.
- **Situational Awareness:** Archie is designed to trigger "Priority Alerts" if certain keywords (like 'Breakthrough' or 'Crisis') are detected in the data feeds.
